Rubber Duck

On a drive back home I found myself desperately trying to hold onto an idea and not forget it, I tried to get siri to record a voice memo, but that failed, then I tried to call myself to record it as a voicemail but that just returned user busy. As I was stuck trying to remember this thought my mind started to drift.

As I thought about voice memos and how they feel more natural in terms of flow compared to that of note-taking and I came up with these main points:

  • I want to verbalise my ideas rather than write them
  • I prefer to read back over my ideas than listen
  • I feel just taking to your phone doesn't help this flow

With these points I came up with this idea of combining the developers current habit of taking over bugs with a rubber duck with the concept of voice memos.

MVP Requirements

  • Record voice memos
  • Send voice memo in text form to my email
  • Replay last voice memo
  • Scrap recording
  • Fit entire concept within a rubber duck
